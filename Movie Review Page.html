Movie Review: The Mummy (1999)

The Mummy is a classic horror movie. The 1999 version builds upon the original, refining it while still paying homage. It's not particularly realistic, but it doesn't need to be. It's got good romance, attractive leads, compelling plot, and more!

Movie Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (10/10)

Cast Members

  • Brendan Fraser as Rick O'Connell
  • Rachel Weisz as Evelyn Carnahan
  • John Hannah as Johnathan Carnahan
  • Arnold Vosloo as The Mummy (Prince Imhotep)
  • Oded Fehr as Ardeth Bay
